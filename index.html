Uganda

Kampala, the capital of Uganda, is a vibrant city located near Lake Victoria. Famous for its seven hills, it beautifully blends traditional and modern cultures. Key landmarks include the Uganda National Mosque and the Independence Monument, highlighting the nation's rich heritage. The lively markets, like Owino Market, offer a glimpse of local life with crafts, fresh produce, and street food. Kampala's nightlife is energetic, featuring diverse restaurants and bars. Green spaces, such as the Kampala Botanical Gardens, provide a peaceful retreat. With its friendly atmosphere and dynamic vibe, Kampala is an essential starting point for exploring Uganda's stunning landscapes and wildlife.
